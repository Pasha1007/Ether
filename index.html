WL FAQ

There is no set criteria for obtaining a WL. We do not encourage “WL grinding” or spamming message counts on the server. Instead, we encourage members to use their unique skills to support the project. This includes (but is not limited to) creating fan art, cosplays, threads, lore, memes, videos, graphics, etc. Viii will handpick and distribute WL as he sees fit. We will also host events where winners can obtain a WL. Events will be infrequent and be announced through the Ether discord or the founders’ twitter accounts. The structure of events and their rules will be detailed in their respective announcements. Keep a lookout for the aforementioned platforms!

Whitelists are distributed through direct messages from the @SpiritEther account. MAKE SURE to follow @SpiritEther on twitter. If you are not following the account we can’t message you, meaning your WL will be voided.
An official Ether member will NEVER send you a link. Ether will NEVER have a stealth mint.
Note: Beware of scams, and ALWAYS verify the authenticity of accounts. If you’ve received a DM from @SpiritEther, ensure the account is followed by both @visionofviii and @CValley_

No, there is no set guideline to guaranteeing yourself a WL. Due to the influx of hard working members who are constantly curating content to show their support, it is not possible to reward every single person.
Making content to show your support DOES NOT guarantee a WL.

A WL role would mean assigning thousands of roles to potential minters, therefore being an inefficient use of the team’s time. The Ether website will have a whitelist checker to verify if you are whitelisted in the system and how many you have.
Note: the Ether website has not launched yet!
